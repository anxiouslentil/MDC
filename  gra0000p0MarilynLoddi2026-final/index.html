Synopsis

San Francisco Detective Valentina Vargas has spent fifteen years rebuilding herself from the inside out. The contaminated meat that killed her family, packaged, shipped, and sold by NutriAxis Corporation took her hair and rewired her body. It also left her with something in return: a palate so precise she can break a single bite down to its ingredients, and identify a toxin at parts per million.

NutriAxis has been headquartered twelve miles from the Mission District her whole life. Nobody has ever answered for what they did to her family. Nobody has even tried.

Until a NutriAxis whistleblower turns up dead on her caseload, ruled accidental, close it fast, don't ask questions.

Valentina asks questions for a living.
